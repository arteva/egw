Research Dust

Dust research integrates both environmental microbes and translate their questions into plant in order to study areas where microbs feel uncomfy. Biotic samples are the qualibush from native grasshoppers land of Tahumo Moa. These plants are tasty for local soups but contain harmful metals that can cause the soup to be explosive mixture. Hence our team investigate the hyperaccumulating aspects of the grass and how to prevent from the spread of the dust in the Picaccue mountains. Using advanced imaging techniques and Synchrotron-based applications we will achieve this soon
