About the Artist

Ewa Gadnicka Wlodarczyk

I have graduated with a master's degree from Krakow Academy of Fine Arts the Faculty of the Graphics. I defended my thesis in the Lithography and Poster Studio in 1987. Since then, I have been professionally involved in graphic design, paints, draws and creates graphic arts. I am engaged in the number of sacred projects and am writing canonical icons. I conduct drawing and watercolor workshops, take part in international painting plein-airs, and have taken part in numerous group exhibitions and was the author of many solo exhibitions. My works are in private collections in Poland, Germany, Austria, Canada, Mexico and South Africa. I am an active member of the Association of Polish Artists and Designers, and the Association of Polish Pastelists.
